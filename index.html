About

I'm a data scientist who specializes in transforming complex data into compelling visual narratives. As an M.S. Analytics candidate at NC State's Institute for Advanced Analytics, I've honed my ability to bridge the gap between technical analysis and business insights, making data accessible and actionable for stakeholders at all levels.

My passion lies at the intersection of data visualization and client collaboration, where I focus on uncovering the story behind the numbers. One of my proudest projects involved developing a comprehensive Tableau dashboard for the NC State Women's tennis team, enhancing recruitment strategies and player performance analysis. This experience exemplifies my commitment to creating data solutions that drive meaningful impact.

I also play a lead role in integrating a data ethics framework into the project, ensuring alignment with industry best practices.

When I'm not diving into datasets, you can find me playing games from the Resident Evil franchise or practicing piano.
